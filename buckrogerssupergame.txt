Buck Rogers Super Game
by Team Pixelboy | 2013

The Buck Rogers space ship is in your hands!
Step into the command seat of the Buck Rogers fighter for a true space adventure! 
Follow Buck's journey as you set out for the Planet of Zoom. 
An alien intelligence has taken control of the area. 
You must get there before the alien force devastates the planet. 
Buck's problems are now your problems: Fleets of alien spacecraft keep you from getting to the planet quickly and safely!

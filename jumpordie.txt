Jump Or Die (2006 Daniel Bienvenu)

Experimentation of a plateform engine with small sprites. You should know how to jump in order to collect all the keys. Coded in a single day, only 1 level.
A one player action game published by Public Domain in 2006, developed by Daniel Bienvenu.

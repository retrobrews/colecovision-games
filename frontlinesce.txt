Front Line S.C.E
by Team Pixelboy | 2015

Uses a standard controller. Test your courage in action-packed combat!
You're a lonely infantryman, fighting through treacherous terrain. The enemy comes at you from all sides. Counterattack with your pistol and grenades before it's too late! The battleground changes constantly. Watch out for deadly land mines! When enemy tanks close in, leap into your own tank! Your ultimate mission: Reach and destroy the enemy's fortress.

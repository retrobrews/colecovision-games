Cosmo Fighter 2 (1997 Marcel de Kogel)

A giant spaceship is threatening to attack the planet Xunor and capture its inhabitants. 
As commander of the mighty Cosmo Fighters, you are given the task of eliminating this evil force and returning peace to the galaxy.
But this mission won’t be an easy one! To reach your goal, you must fight your way through various stages, each filled with advanced enemy fighters, 
grouping together to prevent you from finishing your mission successfully.

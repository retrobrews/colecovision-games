Jeepers Creepers (2007 Daniel Bienvenu)

Description:
It's Halloween Night and your girlfriend has been kidnapped by an evil wizard who wants to lum her into his undead bride. !
Armed with an unlimited supply of axes, you set out the rescue her, but you'll have to face hordes of skeletons, ghosts and green-skined monsters. !
You must kill them all in order to progress towards the wizard's castle. !
Can you save your love ?
This is one halloween night you'll never forget. !

Jeepers Creepers by Daniel Bienvenu was re released on ColecoVision's 30 years Anniversary in 2012 by Team Pixelboy, 20 boxed copies exist.
Thanks to: Sylvain de Chantal, Yannick Proulx, Marcel de Kogel, John Dondzila, Luc Miron and Dale Wick.
A newer version of Jeepers Creepers exist in a black box released via AtariAge.Com from November 2016. 100 boxed copies exist.

Robobug (2012 Nicolas Campion)

Robobug is a ColecoVision program by Nicolas “Nicam Shilova” Campion. The creation of Robobug was basically to get the author into the language C 
and other ColecoVision related development tools. Change the color of the robot, scatter flying objects as you approach them, or changing the 
text displayed at the bottom of the screen.

Controls :
– Stick to move.
– Left side button: Start, blink Robobug.
– Right side button: Start, reset Robobug color.
– KeyPad: Display messages.

Pong (2007 Alfonso DC)

Pong is one of the first computer games that ever created, this simple "tennis like" game features two paddles and a ball, 
the goal is to defeat your opponent by being the first one to gain 10 point, a player gets a point once the opponent 
misses a ball. The game can be played with two human players, or one player against a computer controlled paddle.

Princess Quest (2012 Team Pixelboy)

Your princess has been captured by an evil king!
The legends says that a beautiful princess was born to give happiness to a true knight. An evil king with magical powers kidnapped the
princess, taking her far away to a demonic kingdom. She was made prisoner in a castle. To reach it, a knight must travel without
fear through the darkness. Only a brave knight will be able to defeat the vicious monsters guarding the princess.
